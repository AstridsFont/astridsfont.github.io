Fonts in progress

I have developed the fonts you see here over the years especially for book covers. They are technically functional, but not prepared for the final. They do not offer an extensive glyph collection, but I can quickly compensate for this if necessary.

As you know, kerning is by far the most time-consuming part of font design. The metrics are generally not calculated, as you can easily see. However, this should not be a problem if they are to be used as headlines, as fonts are adjusted manually in the design process anyway.

These are all unique pieces. So far I have neither published, passed on nor sold the fonts. If you are interested in one of these fonts or just want to give me feedback, just write to me.
